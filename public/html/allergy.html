Allergy Awareness and Precautions

Allergies occur when the immune system reacts to a foreign substance such as pollen, pet dander, or certain foods. Understanding triggers and taking precautions can minimize allergic reactions.

Common Allergy Triggers

  • Pollen from trees, grasses, and weeds.
  • Dust mites and mold spores.
  • Animal dander (from pets like cats and dogs).
  • Certain foods like nuts, shellfish, and dairy.
  • Insect stings or bites.

Precautions to Take

  • Avoid known allergens and keep your environment clean.
  • Use air purifiers and maintain proper ventilation.
  • Wash hands and clothes after exposure to outdoor allergens.
  • Carry antihistamines or prescribed medications for emergencies.
  • Inform friends and colleagues about severe food allergies.
