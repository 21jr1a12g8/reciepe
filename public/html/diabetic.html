Diabetes Care and Precautions

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how the body processes blood sugar (glucose). Proper management and care can help you lead a healthy life. Here are some essential precautions:

Precautions for Diabetics

  • Maintain a balanced diet with low sugar and carbs.
  • Exercise regularly to keep your blood sugar levels stable.
  • Monitor your blood sugar levels frequently.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Take prescribed medication or insulin as directed by your doctor.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
  • Schedule regular check-ups with your healthcare provider.

Emergency Symptoms to Watch

  • Severe fatigue or dizziness.
  • Blurred vision or confusion.
  • Excessive thirst or urination.
  • Unhealing wounds or infections.
